  • This is a game where all the players take the role of ideas in the head of a very drunk man trying to return home after a long night of drinking. Each player plans out a single action and then each action is played out in turn order. The player try to work with each other to accomplish various goals and tasks, while also trying to keep the other player from fulfilling their own ideas. The winner is the player with the greatest total of points at the end of the game, which can happen in a number of different ways: if he sobers up, if he falls unconscious, if he manages to get home or if too much time passes and his wife finds him out in the street.

    This is a game for 2 to 4 players, based on the popular game „Sorry“. Instead on nameless markers, players are controlling some of the many available characters with special powers and battling against one of the six different monsters.

    The player who manages to defeat the monster first is the winner. At the start of the game, players choose one of the six unique monsters to be their final enemy – but this also slightly changes the way the game is played, making every game play a different experience. Every player starts the game with three different characters, but during the course of the game they will increase their powers, or swapped for a different character.

  • - is an abstract game of rugby for 2 players. Each player controls a team of 5 pieces, with the goal of gaining control of the ball and moving it over the opponent’s end zone. Similar to games like Chess and Go, players take turns moving one piece at a time as they try to outsmart and outmaneuver the other player to get the best possible position to win. The winner is the first player to pass the opponents end-line with a piece carrying the ball.
