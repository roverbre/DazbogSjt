Stoneball

Stoneball is an abstract game of rugby for 2 players. Each player controls a team of 5 pieces, with the goal of gaining control of the ball and moving it over the opponent’s end zone. Similar to games like Chess and Go, players take turns moving one piece at a time as they try to outsmart and outmaneuver the other player to get the best possible position to win. The winner is the first player to pass the opponents end-line with a piece carrying the ball
