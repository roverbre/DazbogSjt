About us

Dažbog games is an association of board games designers consisting of 4 members. We are based in Belgrade, Serbia (which is, if you are not familliar with it in south-eastern Europe, south of Hungary, west of Romania and north of Greece).

Name: Damjan Miladinović

Place of birth: Belgrade

Date of birth: 08.08.1985

Bio: Graduated from Higher Education School of Professional Studies for Information and Communication Technologies, Bachelor degree, Professional engineer of electronic and computer technologies – specialist for Electronic Business.
Developed many websites and applications for small businesses and Statistical office of Serbia.
Hobbies, aside from gaming include photography, medieval archery and fighting and juggling.
Since childhood have been playing games, both computer and board, and after game mastering bunch of custom rpg campaigns decision to dabble in the field of game design came easy.

Games: To D Island, Long way home, Byte run, Knights journey, Hold my hand, Stranded and Hexopoly

Links: BoardGameGeek

Name: Pavle Ilijašević

Place of birth: Belgrade

Date of birth: 12.08.1989.

Bio: Graduated from the Faculty of Architecture with a Masters degree in architecture. Currently working as a construction engineer in Belgrade Serbia. In his free time, he attends a Historical European Martial Arts swordplay school – TERCA, and is a Boardgame designer in Dažbog Games.
Pavle has always been a gamer, and a great fan of strategy and mystery games. He enjoys both playing and making games in which players must outsmart each other or the game itself.

Games: To D Island, Stoneball, Long way home and Hexopoly.

Links: BoardGameGeek

Name: Vuk Dragičević

Place of birth: Brčko

Date of birth: 19.10.1981.

Bio: 2D and 3D graphical designer/artist.
Finished- Colleague of Fine Arts in Belgrade (ALU), comicbook school “Djordje Lobachev”, 3D school “Chiron”.
Working on games for Ipad and android.
Hobbies: drawing and 3d modelling, sci-fi and fantasy books/games (pc and board), painting miniatures, fighting with longsword (Terca – School of historical European swordplay, Beli Orlovi- medieval association).

Games: To D Island, Hold my hand, Angry Quest, Long way home and Hexopoly.

Links: DeviantArt

Name: Željko Glumac

Place of birth: Belgrade

Date of birth:12.12.1982.

Bio: Graduate School of Business Studies, Megatrend University in Belgrade, 2007. Worked closely with his College as an student-assistant during studies and full time assistant after finished studies. Although it was good he decided to pursue his dreams and to make his hobbies his job. Now he is president and fencing instructor in “School of historical European swordplay – Terca” and game designer associate in Dažbog Games.
Željko is great lover of games and gaming since from forever. He designed his first boardgame at age of 11. Since than he designed boardgames and card games out of hobby and had many ideas for other board, card and computer games. For a long time it was just his hobby, while pursuing other carrier. In recent years he decided to do what he loves the most – to design games. Planer Runners is remake of first game he ever made, where you rolled D6 die for movement and solved fields with items you collected along the way and definite switch in his life and carrier.

Games: Planet Runners, Stoneball Long way home and Hexopoly

Links: BoardGameGeek, Twitch
