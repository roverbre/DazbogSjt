Games

  • This is a game where all the players take the role of ideas in the head of a very drunk man trying to return home after a long night of drinking. Each player plans out a single action and then each action is played out in turn order. The player try to work with each other to accomplish various goals and tasks, while also trying to keep the other player from fulfilling their own ideas. The winner is the player with the greatest total of points at the end of the game, which can happen in a number of different ways: if he sobers up, if he falls unconscious, if he manages to get home or if too much time passes and his wife finds him out in the street.

  • is a strategic economical city building game in which the players take the roles of investors and landowners attempting to profit from their endeavors. Each turn is divided into a city phase and an action phase, where players take turns build houses, selling them off to residents, constructing shopping centers, theaters and schools to influence the game board to earn the most money. Ever building and land deeds the players acquire adds to the end score. The player with the highest score and value of land, buildings and money in the bank (as well as the lowest number of loans which count as negative points) at the end of the game wins as the most successful landowner.

  • The kingdom is under attack by a monster, and who better to save it but an ensemble of heroes! Each player takes a party of heroes and a quest to defeat the monster plaguing the land, while trying to stop the other players from accomplishing the quest before they can. Any player can defeat the guards but the first to defeat the monster itself is the real winner!

  • the player take the rolls of hackers running through codes of data trying to collect what they need while preventing the other players from reaching their goal before they do. The board is set out by tiles with different code element presented on them, and ass the players move their pieces from one space to the other, they perform the various actions on the spaces, reprogramming potentially harmful effects or sending them to effect other players instead. Some spaces grant victory points in the form of data, while running through the complete code grands data based on the number of tiles completed. The first player to reach 10 points of data collected wins.

  • This is a game about survival on a desserted two dimensional island. Players are stickman who try to survive hazards of the island - the vulcano and monster that lives in it and to build a raft and escape together. The game board consists of two boards, between which the game shifts, and that change perspective on items, board and the way of thinking.

  • - is an abstract game of rugby for 2 players. Each player controls a team of 5 pieces, with the goal of gaining control of the ball and moving it over the opponent’s end zone. Similar to games like Chess and Go, players take turns moving one piece at a time as they try to outsmart and outmaneuver the other player to get the best possible position to win. The winner is the first player to pass the opponents end-line with a piece carrying the ball.
